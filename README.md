## Mission

The **Antinomie-Institut** operates as a digital archive exploring the boundaries of automated creativity, algorithmic hallucination ("slop"), and the theological implications of artificial intelligence.

Our research focuses on the **Chain of Custody for Creativity**. We distinguish between biological, synthetic, and hybrid origins of content.
